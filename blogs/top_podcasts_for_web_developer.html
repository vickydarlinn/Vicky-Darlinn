As i got better in my Frontend skills. Then I can share my journey and guidance to all the new comers in the web world.

SYNTAX Syntax is hosted by two of the most popular teachers out there- wes bos and Scott Tolinsky. The podcast visits a ton of different technical topics from typScript to command line tools.

PODROCKETpodrocket is hosted by a trio from LoogRocket and is the newest podcast on this list (at least to me). The PodRocket show does weekly interviews with some pretty large industry profiles, and the questions seem well through out.

The undefined Podcast The undefined podcast is hosted by two of my personal favourites- ken wheeler and jared Palmer. Its clain to frame is that it's the only podcastwith a two drink minimum-a stratergy that gets some really cool discussions going.
